Our Story

Pacific Trails Resorts was built for tourists looking for a closer relationship with the natural world. Our resort, which is tucked away along the untamed California coast, mixes warmth with the raw beauty of nature. Visitors come here to unwind, take a deep breath, and rediscover the delight of discovering the natural world.

What We Offer

  • Hiking excursions with a guide through coastal woods
  • Kayaking adventures along serene coves of the ocean
  • Birdwatching tours led by local naturalists
  • Outdoor yoga classes overlooking the Pacific
  • Private yurts designed for relaxation and introspection
